About Us

Welcome to Aldo Travel, where every journey is filled with excitement, curiosity, and a touch of fun! We’re not your typical travel company — we’re a team of adventurers dedicated to turning your travel dreams into reality.

Aldo - The Playful Ginger Cat & Adventure Enthusiast

Aldo is the heart and soul of Aldo Travel. With her fiery orange fur and a personality full of curiosity and mischief, she's always ready to explore the world—whether it's chasing a new adventure or napping in the sun after a long day of travel planning. Her playful spirit inspires our travels, and she brings joy to everyone who crosses her path.

Rose - The Helpful Younger Sister & Organization Expert

Rose, Aldo’s younger sister, is the brains behind the operation. With her keen attention to detail and an eye for all things efficient, Rose makes sure every travel plan goes smoothly. Whether it's handling bookings, organizing itineraries, or helping Aldo with the latest trip, Rose’s calm and steady approach ensures everything is perfectly planned and stress-free.

Together, they make the perfect team, combining fun, playfulness, and precision to create the best travel experiences!
